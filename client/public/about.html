About Welp

A student crowd-sourced UC Berkeley dining hall review and social networking site, with daily-updated menu selections and comments.
The website will contain dropdowns to choose between one of the four campus dining halls (Crossroads, Clark Kerr, Cafe 3, and Foothill Dining) and meal time (Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner), with users being defaulted to viewing Crossroads at the meal offered at the given time of access.
Users may choose to upvote or downvote each main menu item, in addition to submitting a short review/comment of their experience â€” each of which can also be upvoted/downvoted by other users.
Our features:
- Daily and past menu displays (only the main dishes)
- Upvotes and downvotes for each menu item
- Comments section for daily menu review, with more upvotes
- Icons for vegan/meat
Tech Stack:
- React (frontend)
- Jsdom (backend html web scraping & parsing)
- Axios (connecting frontend with backend)
- Express (backend api)
